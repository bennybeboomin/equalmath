Our Mission

EqualMath provides free, personalized math tutoring to students from low-income backgrounds, refugee communities, or anyone lacking access to quality math education. Our volunteer tutors are passionate high school and college students who want to make a difference.

We believe that every student deserves access to quality math education, regardless of their socioeconomic background. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap and empower students through mathematics.

How It Works

Our simple 4-step process makes getting math help easy and effective:

1

Sign Up

Students (or parents/teachers) fill out a simple sign-up form to request tutoring help. It takes less than 2 minutes!

2

Get Matched

We carefully match students with a qualified tutor based on their grade level, learning needs, and availability.

3

Learn Weekly

Students attend free weekly Zoom sessions with their tutor, receiving personalized support and mentorship.

4

Grow and Thrive
